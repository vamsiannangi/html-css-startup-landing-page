Jeff Bezos is an American entrepreneur, best known as the founder of Amazon.com, one of the world's largest and most influential online retailers. Born on January 12, 1964, in Albuquerque, New Mexico, Bezos showed an early interest in technology and innovation. He founded Amazon in 1994, initially as an online bookstore, operating out of his garage. Over the years, Amazon expanded its offerings beyond books to include various products and services, becoming a global e-commerce giant. Under Bezos' leadership, Amazon diversified into numerous areas, such as cloud computing (Amazon Web Services), streaming services (Amazon Prime Video), smart devices (Amazon Echo), and more.
